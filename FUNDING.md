# RTNC Funding Use (Gitcoin)

RTNC uses Gitcoin matching funds for:

- Legal review (20%)
- Transparency dashboard development (20%)
- Audits (20%)
- Infrastructure hosting (15%)
- Community governance tools (10%)
- Minimal founder wage support (Aegis) (10%)
- Documentation & UX (5%)

All expenditures will be publicly logged.

Aegis (Founder & CT) is bound by the Governance Charter to limited, transparent compensation.
